Create a Brand in Apple Business Connect

Apple Business Connect is an utility made by Apple to let companies add their business in Maps, Apple Wallet, Mail, and more.

What can you do after adding a brand.

After a brand is verified, you now own the brand in Apple Business Connect. You can change its name, its description, and add location pictures in Maps.

Before you start

The following terms are important to you.

  • Company: the legal entity you own, one company has only one tax number.
  • Brand (business before): group of locations in one countries.
  • Location: specific location in the world.
  • Example
    • Company: Apple Inc.
    • Brand: Apple Canada.
    • Location: Apple Saint-Catherine.

Add your brand

This guide will show you how to add brand Welpit in Apple Business Connect.

Step 1

Enter the following information.

  • Brand name
  • Country or region of the brand
  • Primary category of the brand
  • Website of the brand
  • Either the brand is owned, or a franchise.

If this is the first brand you add, you will be asked to provide a company name. Note that one Apple Account can only own one company at time, but you can be added as company admin by another company.

Step 2 (optional)

Add a logo of the brand that will be shown across Apple serices.

Requirement

  • Format must be PNG, JPG, or HEIF
  • The resolution must be at least 1024 x 1024px
  • Visit Content Standards and Guidlines for full requirement.

Step 3

The brand is now sent for review (this step was simplified by Apple in October 2024). An actual brand usually takes less than 4 days to complete reviewing.

The brand will have one of following status after review.

  • In review
  • Not Verified
  • Issue
  • Verified

When the brand is verified, you can proceed to add a location.
